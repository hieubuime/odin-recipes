Pho

Description

Pho is a Vietnamese soup dish consisting of broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat (usually beef). Pho is a popular food in Vietnam where it is served in households, street stalls and restaurants countrywide.

Ingredients

  • Marrow bones and Meaty bones
  • Thinly sliced raw meat
  • Yellow onions
  • Whole garlic
  • Fresh ginger
  • Whole spices: star anise, cloves, cinnamon sticks, black cardamom pods, fennel seeds, coriander seeds
  • Salt
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Boil the water and cook the bones, onions, garlic and spices for 8 hours
  2. Boil the beef (brisket, round eye), and then slice thin
  3. Cook the noodles according to packaging instruction
  4. Seasoning the bone broth with salt, sugar, and fish sauce according to taste
  5. Pour the broth in a bowl, add the cooked noodles and the beef
  6. Garnish with green onions and serve
